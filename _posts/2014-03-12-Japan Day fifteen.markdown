Our second last day was spent in [Shimokitazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shimokitazawa) which is a very "trendy" place. It contains of old buildings, lots of art and café after café. In other words: the perfect spot for us.

<!-- more -->

![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121213.jpg)
We started the day with a beautiful cup of cappucino at [Bear Pond](http://www.bear-pond.com) coffee which was equally tasty.

![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121314.jpg)
Then, we continued to [J.S. Pancake Café](http://www.jinlovestoeat.com/2014/07/js-pancake-cafe.html).
![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121255.jpg)
They offered delicious sweet and salty pancakes. Naturally, I went with the sweet pancakes.
![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121256.jpg)
Sophie took salmon avocado pancakes.

We spent some time walking through Shimokitazawa and enjoying the gloomy, artsy mood.
![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121355.jpg)
![Picture](https://f001.backblaze.com/file/unpictures/2014-japan2400/06121455.jpg)
