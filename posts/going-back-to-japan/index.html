Going back to Japan

24 Apr 2023 | 1 min read

In about 60 hours from now, I will take a plane to Japan. It has been about seven years since I last stepped foot in this beautiful country. This will be my fourth time to go to Japan. I am eager to find out, what has changed and what stayed the same.

I am super excited to go back and will report on the journey as I go. Follow along if you care to get some updates.
